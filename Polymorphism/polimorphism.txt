Polymorphism :- The term Polymorphism means one thing many forms . It is two types :-
1. Method Overloading
2. Method Overriding

1) Method Overloading :- If we perform many tasks from a single method then it is called method overloading .

2) Method Overriding :-The re-writing of base class method to derived class is called method Overriding
